# Data Visualization with MDX

Presenting data effectively is crucial for making informed decisions. This example demonstrates how to integrate various data visualization techniques into your MDX documents.

## Simple Chart Components

MDX allows you to embed interactive charts directly in your content:

<Chart 
  type="bar" 
  title="Monthly Revenue"
/>

<Box className="p-4 border rounded-lg mb-6 bg-gray-50 dark:bg-gray-800">
  <h3 className="font-bold mb-3">Sales Comparison</h3>
  <p className="mb-2">Comparison of quarterly sales data (2022-2023)</p>
  
  <div className="grid grid-cols-2 gap-4">
    <Chart type="bar" title="Q1-Q2" />
    <Chart type="bar" title="Q3-Q4" />
  </div>
</Box>

## Best Practices for Data Visualization

<TwoColumnLayout>
  <Column>
    <h3 className="font-bold mb-2">Effective Visualization Tips</h3>
    
    <ul className="list-disc pl-5">
      <li>Choose the right chart type for your data</li>
      <li>Use color purposefully and consistently</li>
      <li>Don't distort the data with misleading scales</li>
      <li>Label data points clearly and directly</li>
      <li>Provide context with titles and legends</li>
      <li>Keep visualizations simple and focused</li>
      <li>Use interactive elements for complex datasets</li>
    </ul>
  </Column>
  
  <Column>
    <h3 className="font-bold mb-2">Common Mistakes to Avoid</h3>
    
    <ul className="list-disc pl-5">
      <li>Using inappropriate chart types</li>
      <li>Cluttering visualizations with too much data</li>
      <li>Relying on 3D charts that can distort perception</li>
      <li>Using inconsistent scales across comparisons</li>
      <li>Choosing confusing or misleading colors</li>
      <li>Omitting important context or methodology</li>
      <li>Failing to make visualizations accessible</li>
    </ul>
  </Column>
</TwoColumnLayout>

## Conclusion

Effective data visualization transforms raw data into actionable insights. By leveraging MDX's component-based approach, you can create rich, interactive data visualizations that enhance understanding and drive better decision-making.

<Callout type="tip">
  Remember that the goal of data visualization is to communicate clearly, not to impress with complexity. Always prioritize clarity and accuracy over visual flair.
</Callout>
